📌 Sticky Scroll

Pin any element while scrolling

Active Elements: 0
Selection Mode: Off

How to use:

  1. Click "Toggle Selection Mode" or the 🎯 button on the page
  2. Choose your mode: 📝 Sticky Notes or 📌 Sticky Scroll
  3. Hover over any element you want to pin
  4. Sticky Notes: Creates draggable floating notes
  5. Sticky Scroll: Pins elements to top of page

Two Modes:

  • 📝 Sticky Notes: Copy content into draggable notes
  • 📌 Sticky Scroll: Pin elements to top while scrolling
  • ✅ Works on all websites including PDFs
  • ✅ Visual indicators on original elements
  • ✅ Easy management with clear buttons
