Privacy Policy for Sticky Scroll Extension

Last Updated: January 2025

TL;DR: This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Data Collection

The Sticky Scroll Extension does not collect any personal data. We do not:

  • Collect personally identifiable information
  • Track your browsing history
  • Monitor your web activity
  • Access your personal communications
  • Collect location data
  • Store financial or health information

2. Local Storage

The extension stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Pinned element positions and content for sticky scroll functionality
  • Sticky note content that you create
  • Extension preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

3. Permissions

The extension requires these permissions for functionality:

  • activeTab: To inject content scripts for element selection
  • storage: To save your pinned elements locally
  • host permissions: To work on any website you choose to use it on

4. Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services. No data is sent to external servers, analytics services, or tracking platforms.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any data because we don't collect any data in the first place.

6. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage APIs, your data security depends on your device's security and Chrome's local storage protection.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be posted here. Since we don't collect contact information, we cannot notify users directly of changes.

8. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store developer contact information.

Summary: Your privacy is completely protected because we don't collect anything. This extension works entirely on your device with no external connections.
